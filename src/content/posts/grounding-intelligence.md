### I. The Research Gap

**Beyond the Notebook**
The transition from a Jupyter notebook to a scalable API requires a fundamental shift from optimizing for accuracy to optimizing for intent and reliability.

**Data as Infrastructure**
Models are only as good as the pipelines that feed them. Real-world grounding requires a living data strategy that accounts for drift and noise.

### II. Observability as Feature

**Interpretable Latency**
It's not just about how fast the system responds, but how clearly it communicates the confidence and reasoning behind its output.

**The Feedback Loop**
Building systems that learn from user interaction without sacrificing stability is the core challenge of modern AI architecture.
